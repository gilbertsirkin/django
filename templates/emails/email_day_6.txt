Subject: You're earning — here's how to earn more

Hey there,

Once you have a fixed-yield plan running, you'll start accumulating regular WOLV tokens on your user dashboard. But what do you do with those tokens? 

You can let them sit, you can move them to an external wallet, or you can utilize our Direct Staking Dashboard to double-dip on your allocations.

Direct staking allows you to take your accumulated WOLV tokens (or extra BNB) and lock them directly into our dedicated token liquidity contracts. 

For example: An investor runs a $1,000 Horizon plan, earning steady fixed distributions. They take their daily WOLV payouts and route them immediately into the direct staking protocol. They also stake $200 BNB separately. They receive ~$90 APY gain at maturity plus daily WOLV rewards on the $200 staking position — two separate payouts, from the same account, running in parallel.

The staking dashboard is live in your account. You don't need to wait for your investment plan to mature to access it.
