One thing that surprises new investors: WOLV rewards don't sit in a WolvCapital account. They're sent to your wallet — MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or any BEP-20 compatible address you provide. That means you own them outright, from day one.

If you haven't connected a wallet yet, here's exactly how:

1
Open MetaMask or Trust Wallet
Any BEP-20 compatible wallet works. Make sure you're on BNB Smart Chain (not Ethereum mainnet).
↓
2
Add WOLV as a custom token
In MetaMask: Assets → Import Token → paste the contract address below. Trust Wallet detects it automatically.
↓
3
Connect your wallet in dashboard
Go to your WolvCapital dashboard → Wallet → Connect. Your WOLV balance syncs in real time.
↓
4
Claim any time you're eligible
Hit "Claim WOLV" in your dashboard. Tokens transfer directly to your wallet address — no withdrawal form, no wait time.
