Subject: Here's everything we can't hide (and why that's good)

Hey there,

In decentralized finance, security isn't about branding; it's about architecture. When we launched our updated smart contracts, we built them to ensure that no single person — not even our development team — can alter the core payout mechanics once your position is active.

Here is exactly what is hardcoded into our system:

- Immutable Lock Periods: Once your plan is active, the timelines are fixed. No early clawbacks, no unexpected fee alterations.
- Audited Codebases: Our smart contracts have undergone extensive code reviews to minimize structural vulnerabilities.
- Non-custodial WOLV rewards: WOLV tokens are paid directly to your connected wallet address. The contract handles distributions programmatically; we don't hold your reward tokens on your behalf.

None of this removes market risk — crypto is volatile and staking carries principal risk. But it does mean the platform itself is structured to be transparent, not just trustworthy-sounding.
