01 — HOW SCAMS ACTUALLY WORK

They promise returns. They hide the mechanism.

Every fraudulent investment platform has one thing in common — they tell you what you'll earn but never explain how. The mechanism is always vague. "Proprietary trading algorithm." "Expert fund managers." "AI-powered returns."

When something is genuinely real, the mechanism is explainable. It can be shown to you. It can be verified independently.

The question to ask any platform:

"Can I verify this independently, without trusting you?"

If the answer is no — walk away.
